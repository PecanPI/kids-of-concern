Canadian author, Perry Logan, woke up on the couch one day with the idea for Kids of Concern. He got up and started writing. Kids of Concern is a North American political adventure novel that tries hard to stay ahead of the real-life plot twists happening today. Backroom politics, espionage, scandals, and futuristic technology all combine in this face paced novel that takes you on an adventure where the kids are not the only concern.
